Hello, I’m Chris!

I’m an interaction designer based in Leeds and I design accessible services that real people can use.

I currently work for HSE looking at public facing services and how our users interact with the organisation. Helping users get the advice or outcome that they need.

As part of my time at HSE I have:

  • helped to promote united patterns across services using the Gov design principles
  • helped the organisation move towards a unified approach of user-centred design

Other experiences I have include:

  • helping teams across HMRC meet standards and use the best pattern to meet user needs
  • assessing services against the Government Design Principles (previously the GDS standards)
